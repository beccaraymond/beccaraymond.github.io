UF INVOLVEMENT

ReadingPals Mentor

2015-2016

ReadingPals is a program offered through United Way of Central Florida and matches 2nd and 3rd grade students with volunteers to read with them one-on-one for one school year. Volunteers serve as mentors and tutors with these students (who have been identified as needing help with reading).


Gator Teens Mentor

2014-2015

Gator Teens is one of the programs in the MentorUF program offered by the Center for Leadership and Service at the University of Florida. Gator Teens mentors provide at-risk high school students in Gainesville with access to resouources and advice on pursuring post-secondary education.


Project MASCOT Mentor

2013-2014

Project MASCOT (Mentoring Alliance: Students and Communities Outreaching Together) is another program in the MentorUF program at the Center for Leadership and Service at UF. It matches volunteer mentors with at-risk elementary school students who may need help with schoolwork or benefit from mentoring in another capacity.
